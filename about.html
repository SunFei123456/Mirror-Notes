Why We Created This Website

In today's social media era, appearance anxiety has become a global mental health issue. The proliferation of filters, photo editing, and "perfect" images has led more and more people into deep insecurity about their appearance.

Mirror Notes' Mission: To provide an anonymous, warm space where everyone can freely express their appearance anxieties. When you see that others share the same insecurities, you'll discoverâ€”you are not alone.
